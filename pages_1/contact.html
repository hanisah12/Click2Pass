Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I apply for a bus pass?

Click on "Apply Pass" in the navigation menu, fill out the form with your details, upload required documents, and proceed to payment.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid ID proof, passport size photo, and category proof (student ID/employee ID) for discount eligibility.

How long does delivery take?

Physical passes are delivered within 3-5 business days. Digital passes are available immediately after payment confirmation.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if the pass hasn't been activated. Processing fee is non-refundable.

Is auto-renewal mandatory?

No, auto-renewal is optional. You can enable or disable it anytime from your account settings.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.
